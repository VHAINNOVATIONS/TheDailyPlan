The Daily Plan® was initiated to increase patient safety during hospitalization by empowering patients - and their families - to ask questions about their care; in particular, to speak up if they believe a patient's care plan is different than expected.

The plan is a patient-specific document reviewed with patients each day of their hospitalization.
